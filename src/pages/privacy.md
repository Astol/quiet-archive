# Privacy Policy

This is a personal website. I keep data collection as limited as possible.

## What data may be processed

This site may process basic technical information such as IP address, browser information, request logs, and timestamps through hosting and security providers.

If authentication, forms, or admin features are used, additional data such as email address and submitted content may be stored.

## Why data is processed

Data is processed to:
- keep the site running
- protect the site from abuse
- enable login/admin functionality
- respond to messages, if contact forms are used
- understand basic site usage, if analytics are enabled

## Third-party services

This site may rely on Cloudflare, Supabase, and GitHub to host, secure, deploy, and operate the website. These services may process limited technical or account-related data when needed for the site to function.

Personal data is not sold.

## Cookies

This site does not use non-essential cookies unless stated otherwise.

If analytics or third-party embeds are added later, this policy will be updated.
